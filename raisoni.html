A Few Words About GHRIET

Established in 2008, GHRIET is an Autonomous Institute affiliated with Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University, Nagpur. Recognized by AICTE and accredited by NAAC with A+ grade, GHRIET is part of the Raisoni Group of Institutions (RGI), dedicated to enlightening minds and empowering ambitions.
